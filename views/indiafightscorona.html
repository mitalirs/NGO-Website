"#IndiaFightsCorona"

On 11 March 2020, World Health Organization (WHO) declared Novel Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) outbreak as a pandemic and reiterated the call for countries to take immediate actions and scale up response to treat, detect and reduce transmission to save people’s lives.
The Prime Minister's Office, Group of Ministers, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare and Cabinet Secretary are closely monitoring the situation. Addressing the nation on 19 March 2020, the Prime Minister, urged for resolve and restraint, and adopting social distancing, including ‘National Lockdown’ for 21 days starting Wednesday, 25 March 2020. However, due to a rise in cases of Covid-19 in various pockets, on 14 April the Prime Minister extended the nationwide lockdown till 3 May. Since the lockdown CHILDLINE has answered more than 4.26 Lakh calls and sent on ground emergency responders to more than 9000+ children across the country.
We at CHILDLINE 1098 urge you to continue your support and help us keep our helpline operational 24X7 and for our teams to continue reaching out to rescue more children in distress and in need of help.
